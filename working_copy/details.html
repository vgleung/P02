New Heights. New Fears. New Places.

When you’re in gameworld, the audiovisual effect fools your brain into believing that you’re there. You don’t need to approach photo-realism to deliver presence, as long as you’re providing a consistent world. The Climb, we really push the visuals to bring you this experience. Our gameplay in environments were inspired by areas of outstanding beauty around the world, so people could experience what it’s like to be high up on a rock face in the Alps or in Halong Bay. Why not take players to the most spectacular places you can?
